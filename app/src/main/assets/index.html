ABOUT CDNET

CDNet (Career Development Network) is a duly registered indigenous not for profit, non-governmental organisation whose objective is to research, develop and organise appropriate data, relevant information and resource materials for the provision of career development services for the benefit of secondary school students, young school leavers, undergraduates and job seekers to address youth un-employability and unemployment.

ABOUT I CEE TECH

The programme I Cee Tech is currently in its third year (as at 2016). For two consecutive years, CDNet has partnered with leading IT companies to run a 3-day learning event to provide Nigerian youths selected through rigorous screening process, with ICT industry-specific employability and entrepreneurial knowledge and skills.
